
Keyboard controls are only active when your mouse cursor is over the viewport window.

- Hold: SHIFT + RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON
  -> When Dragging mouse rotate the skybox lighting

- Hold: ALT + W
  -> Move camera forward;
- Hold: ALT + A
  -> Move camera left;
- Hold: ALT + S
  -> Move camera back;
- Hold: ALT + D
  -> Move camera right;
